gravity, attraction force between objects
friction, motion resistance from objects rubbing against each other
displacement, the change in an object's position
impulse, change in momentum
pressure, force pushing on an object
momentum, measurement of mass in motion
anion, a negatively charged ion
inertia, tendency of an object to resist any change in its motion
electricity, the movement of electrons between atoms
velocity, the speed and the direction of motion of an object
acceleration, the rate at which the velocity of a body changes with time
sound, mechanical wave composed of frequencies within the range of human hearing
photon, the quantum of light and all other forms of electromagnetic radiation
atom, basic unit of matter that consists of a central nucleus surrounded by a cloud of electrons
dielectric, an electrical insulator that can be polarized by an applied electric field
